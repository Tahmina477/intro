About Academy
Alif Academy is a non-profit organization that conducts educational programs, events and consultations to promote the information technology sector in Tajikistan. We develop IT education with the belief that it will give people a new craft and help them earn a decent living at home without leaving the country to work. Alif Academy - has been working in Dushanbe and Khujand since 2017. During its existence, the academy has released more than 60 students to the market, of which 70% found a job and found their calling.
